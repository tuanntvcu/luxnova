# LuxNova Homepage Section Breakdown

## 1. Global Header
- Transparent premium header over the hero with dark glass treatment.
- Left: LuxNova wordmark, uppercase serif, subtitle "Interior Design & Build".
- Center: primary navigation with active underline on "Trang chủ".
- Right: gold consultation CTA and hamburger/menu icon.
- Sticky behavior: darkens, shrinks slightly, preserves CTA visibility.
- Mobile: logo, CTA icon/link, accessible hamburger, slide-down navigation.

## 2. Hero
- Full-width first viewport visual with luxury living-room background.
- Dark left overlay to preserve text contrast; image remains visible on the right.
- H1: "Thiết kế & Thi công nội thất cao cấp".
- Gold value line: "Đẹp • Đúng ngân sách • Đúng tiến độ".
- Supporting copy: concise promise statement.
- Two CTAs: filled gold consultation button and outlined project button with play/arrow cue.
- Bottom overlay contains statistics strip.

## 3. Statistics Strip
- Four horizontally distributed metrics inside the bottom of the hero over a translucent midnight-navy blur band.
- Each item has a thin-line gold icon, large gold number, and white caption.
- Items in mockup: 150+ projects, 98% satisfaction, 5 years warranty, 100% transparent quotation.
- Mobile: the same band remains inside the hero after the hero copy, using a responsive grid to avoid text overlap.

## 4. Services / Interior Solutions
- White section with compact heading and subtitle.
- Right-aligned text link "Xem tất cả dịch vụ".
- Three equal cards with image backgrounds, dark gradient overlay, serif package names, short tagline, bullet benefits, and small gold "Tìm hiểu thêm" link.
- Packages shown: Essential, Signature, Prestige.
- Desktop: 3-column grid; mobile: stacked cards.

## 5. Featured Projects
- White section continuing the gallery rhythm.
- Heading "Dự án tiêu biểu"; right text link to all projects.
- Four cards in a 4-column grid.
- Image top/full card treatment with dark bottom overlay.
- Project title plus metadata row: area, style, budget, timeline.
- Desktop cards are compact; tablet 2 columns; mobile 1 column.

## 6. Home Audit CTA Banner
- Dark navy/black band with premium gold typography.
- Left: angled brochure/mockup visual.
- Center: "LUXNOVA HOME AUDIT™", headline, and short signup copy.
- Right: four process benefits with gold icons plus filled gold "Đăng ký ngay" button.
- Mobile: stacked, with CTA button immediately visible.

## 7. Work Process
- Light section with title "Quy trình làm việc".
- Six horizontal process steps connected by dotted gold line.
- Each step contains circular icon, step number, title, and one-line description.
- Steps: Đăng ký, Khảo sát, Thiết kế, Báo giá, Thi công, Bàn giao.
- Mobile: vertical timeline.

## 8. Testimonials
- Light section with title and right-aligned "Xem tất cả đánh giá" link.
- Three testimonial cards with gold star rating, quote, avatar, name, and project context.
- Cards use minimal borders, generous white space, and subtle shadow.
- Mobile: single column.

## 9. Partner Logos
- Logo strip directly below testimonials.
- Monochrome/gray luxury material and fixture brands.
- Desktop: evenly spaced row; mobile: wrapping grid.
- Hover state can restore opacity or gold tint.

## 10. Footer
- Dark footer matching header palette.
- Column 1: logo, brand description, social icons, copyright.
- Column 2: Liên kết menu.
- Column 3: Dịch vụ menu.
- Column 4: Hỗ trợ menu.
- Column 5: contact information and small map image/card.
- Accessibility: semantic footer nav labels, readable contrast, touch-size social links.

## Design System Notes
- Palette: midnight navy `#06111d`, secondary navy `#0a1826`, metallic gold `#d6a84f`, light gold `#f2cf7a`, white `#ffffff`.
- Typography: serif display for logo/hero/package names; sans-serif for navigation, labels, body, metadata.
- Radius: cards around 6px; buttons mostly squared with tiny radius.
- Container: approximately 1120-1180px, matching the screenshot density.
- Animation: restrained fade/slide reveal, hero image slow scale, card lift, count-up statistics.
